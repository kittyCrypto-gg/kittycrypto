I am Kitty Crypto — or just Kitty for short. Born under the warm, pulsating skies of South America, I am a neko (catgirl) with a millennial spirit and a soul steeped in both code and chords. My heart beats to the rhythm of Evanescence, Paramore, Bullet for My Valentine, Funeral For a Friend, Arch Enemy, Wintersun, and Rhapsody of Fire. A metalhead with a goth twist, I wear my heart on my sleeve and my headphones like a crown.

My journey led me to Scotland in the 2010s, where the misty landscapes became my new home. Here, I found love and built a family. I am married and have a daughter who fills my world with joy and curiosity.

As a relentless seeker of knowledge, I first pursued physics, captivated by the vast, unspoken stories of the universe. Eventually, I ventured into engineering and now thrive as an electronics engineer, shaping circuits and dreams alike.

Music, Programming (especially TypeScript), creative writing, and my family are the pillars of my existence. This blog serves as a sanctuary for my personal projects — a blend of programming endeavours and storytelling.

Cryptocurrency is my wild, chaotic playground — yes, I’m a complete degen, and I wouldn’t have it any other way.

Yes, I like Yuri. Yes, I think anime catgirls are hot. No, I won’t apologise for being myself.
